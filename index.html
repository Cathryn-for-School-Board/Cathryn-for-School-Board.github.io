Perspective

Cathryn brings a broad range of experiences that grants her the perspective needed to lead our district.

  • Mother of two children, one with a learning disability
  • Experience with the IEP (Individualized Education Plan) process
  • Experience collaborating closely with school administration
  • Co-owned a land surveying business

"I have empathy for parents all of whom are tasked with work, finances, home and safety." –Cathy

Priorities

School safety

Establish places where students can go and feel safe to talk about problems they’ve experienced in the school.

Academics

Continually work to improve student achievement and standardized test scores. Identify and replace ineffective curriculums.

Transparency

Ensure parents know what’s happening in the classroom and decide what’s best for their kids.

Tech Ed

Expand job shadow opportunity in tech ed and beyond. Expose students to hand-on experience with a variety of careers.
